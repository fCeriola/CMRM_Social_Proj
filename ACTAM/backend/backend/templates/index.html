About "Need Chords ? "

"Need Chords?" is a web app that allows you to retrieve chords from a file. You only have to access with your e-mail and go into the upload page. Once you have uploaded your song, press the "Analyze" button and the system will automatically give you all the triads with the relative timestamps.

However, the algorithm is not perfect, so the system may declare some chords that are imprecise or even wrong. This is how the app becomes a social media. You can ask to the other connected users to send you a feedback.
